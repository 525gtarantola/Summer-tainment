Some of my personal favorite restaurants of NYC!

Panorama Room, Roosevelt Island

Though Panorama Room won't put you as close to the waves as some waterside spots, it has one of the most beautiful views your dedicated dining out dollars can buy. Its brief menu is mostly seafood-centric, and a full complement of drinks is available. For more information, click here ⇽

Westlight, Greenpoint

The 22nd floor of the William Vale hotel has a wraparound terrace ideally situated for outdoor dining, drinks and cynicism-smashing views. Visit on a not-too-hot day, order an effervescent cocktail and lower your shades for a nourishing look at the NYC skyline. For more information, click here ⇽

Celestine, DUMBO

A sparkling riverside Mediterranean-influenced restaurant in the shadow of the handsome Manhattan Bridge, Celestine is close enough to the water to hear the waves dancing with the shore. Its terrace frames all that in its tableau, plus a nice wide expanse of the Manhattan skyline. For more information, click here ⇽

Pilot, Brooklyn Heights

Somewhat understated, Pilot is one of the most fashionable boat bars in Brooklyn or beyond. Its wooden finishes and navy color scheme have pops of gloss with the occasional varnished surface and one cheery splash overhead in the white and yellow awning shading the bar. But even a 147-foot schooner crowds pretty quick once people start filing in for oysters, soft-shell crabs and cocktails, so go before sunset to beat the crowds. For more information, click here ⇽
